============================
Licensing and Open Source 
============================

Is Loom an open source project?
-------------------------------
It is not currently. It's being built under Apache 2.0 license with the intent of 
open sourcing it in near future.

When will Loom be open sourced?
-------------------------------
As of now, we are thinking of open sourcing Loom in H1 2014.

What aspects of Loom will be open sourced?
------------------------------------------
Loom Server, Loom UI, Loom Provisioner and plugins for Openstack, Joyent, Rackspace
along with Automator that use Chef. Along with this we will also be open sourcing 
Hadoop cookbok through Chef repo (previously known as Opscode).

What type of license will Loom be open sourced under?
-----------------------------------------------------
Under Apache 2.0 license.
